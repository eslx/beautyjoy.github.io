The Mission  

Our Mission is to substantially increase the supply of effective English language programs for California’s limited English proficient (LEP) adult population. Closing this gap will enable individuals in this population segment to:

  1. broaden their economic opportunities,
  2. advance the academic fortunes of their school-age children; and
  3. participate in the civic activities essential to the maintenance of a vibrant democratic order.

The effort will begin in Santa Clara and San Mateo counties (“Silicon Valley”), and the lessons learned will then be applied to supporting LEP adults in the remaining six counties that constitute the Silicon Valley Bay Area (SVBA), and subsequently in the remainder of the State

The effort will initially benefit Spanish-speaking LEP adults. This group is by far the largest segment in California’s population of ‘Speakers of languages other than English’ (SLOTE).
